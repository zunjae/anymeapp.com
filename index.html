Features

Sync

Synchronize your favorite shows by intergating your MyAnimeList account with AnYme.

Information

View different information about your anime, including voice actors, theme songs, average score, et cetera.

Recommendation

By intergating your MyAnimeList account, you could view other users' recommendations who watched your anime.

Discover

Discover new animes, by using AnYme's random button or via the discovery page.

Discuss

Join discussions about your favorite anime with other fans on reddit.

Schedule

View the dates in which your favorite anime releases episodes.
